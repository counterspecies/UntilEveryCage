Until Every Cage is Empty

Their Power is Secrecy.

The animal agriculture industry's power is built on a foundation of two things: propaganda and secrecy. The propaganda is the myth of the happy farm, the comforting label, the illusion of choice. The secrecy is the brutal, systematic reality behind the walls you see on this map. We are here to shatter both.

This is not a directory; it is a blueprint of an industrial atrocity. An engine of agony is operating around the clock, and its primary fuel is public ignorance. Every year, it consumes billions of lives. 9 billion chickens are shackled, dragged through electrified water, and have their throats cut by automated blades—many are still conscious as they are plunged into scalding water to remove their feathers. 120 million pigs, intelligent beings, are forced into gas chambers where they thrash and scream as carbon dioxide forms carbonic acid in their eyes, nostrils, and lungs, burning them from the inside out. 30 million cows are shot in the head with a steel bolt and hoisted by one leg to have their throats slit, bleeding out on a factory floor slick with the blood of those who went before them. This is the gruesome reality they spend millions to keep from your eyes.

Do not use their words. They are not "livestock." They are not "product." They are not "resources." These are weapons of propaganda—carefully engineered euphemisms designed to erase the individual and sanitize the violence. The beings trapped in this system are sentient individuals, each with a profound will to live. To call them anything else is to participate in the lie.

And do not fall for their most effective piece of propaganda: the myth of "humane" treatment. "Humane slaughter" is a marketing term, a grotesque contradiction designed to soothe your conscience so you continue to buy their products. A bigger cage is a more spacious prison cell. A quicker death is still a violent, unwanted end to a life. You cannot "humanely" take something that is not yours to take.

This system of organized violence has no moral defense. It is a tyrannical machine that profits from terror. The only ethical response is its complete and total abolition.

Your passive observation ends now. Knowledge requires a response. Inaction is complicity. Your duty is to become an active agent in the destruction of this industry's power.

  1. REJECT THE PROPAGANDA. The most powerful way to fight their lies is to stop consuming them. You must go vegan. This is the non-negotiable first step. It is the act of removing your financial support from their entire system of violence.

  2. DESTROY THE SECRECY. Share this map. Show it to everyone you know. Talk about what happens inside these facilities. Use the correct words: slaughter, violence, victim. Drag their hidden atrocities into the light. Their power depends on your silence. Break it.

    3. 3. BEAR WITNESS. Your education in this hidden reality is not optional. You must see what they fight to keep from you. Explore these essential resources to understand the true scope of the horror.

    Foundational Documentaries

    Begin with the unflinching cinematic evidence in Dominion (2018), its foundational predecessor Earthlings (2005), and the environmentally-focused Cowspiracy (2014). These films are a necessary, though difficult, starting point.

    Essential Speeches

    Listen to the passionate and uncompromising arguments that have awakened millions. Gary Yourofsky's 2010 address at Georgia Tech is considered one of the most impactful speeches on animal liberation ever given.

    Key Websites for Further Research

    • ADAPTT: The organization founded by Gary Yourofsky, full of resources and essays.
    • NutritionFacts.org: For those interested in the health aspects, Dr. Michael Greger provides a strictly science-based, evidence-backed analysis of plant-based nutrition.

  3. JOIN THE UPRISING. Your individual actions are vital, but organized resistance is what will bring this empire down. Find an activist organization—local or national—and give them your time and your energy. True justice requires organized, relentless, and direct confrontation.

    • Direct Action Everywhere (DxE): A global grassroots network that uses nonviolent direct action and community building to achieve animal liberation. They have chapters in most major cities.
    • The Save Movement: An international network of groups that hold peaceful vigils at slaughterhouses to "bear witness" to farmed animals in their final moments.
    • Anonymous forthe Voiceless (AV): A global activist movement that organizes 'Cubes of Truth'—silent, static demonstrations showing footage of what happens to animals—to challenge public perception and advocate for veganism.
    • BAAL - (Local Example): An example of a local, grassroots community organizing for abolitionist-focused action in their city.
    • You can contribute directly to this project by emailing untileverycageproject@protonmail.com. Any and all help is greatly appreciated.

To those who own and operate this machinery of death: We see you. We see your facilities, we know your supply chains, we read your shareholder reports. Your empire is built on a foundation of lies and blood, and we will not rest until it has been turned to dust.

We are not asking for reform. We are working for your total obsolescence.

Until every cage is empty.
