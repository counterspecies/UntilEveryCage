Until Every Cage is Empty

Behind the silent walls of concrete and steel, a war is being waged against the innocent. This is not a metaphor. It is a systematic, industrial-scale extermination, hidden from public view but happening every second of every day.

The machine of this industry is vast, but it is not invisible. It is comprised of thousands of facilities, each a node in a chain of suffering. In the United States alone, this machine extinguishes over 9 billion chickens every single year. More than 120 million pigs and 30 million cows meet the same fate. They are born into confinement, live in darkness, and die in terror.

They are individuals, each with a will to live, a capacity for fear, and a desire for freedom. Yet they are treated as mere resourcesâ€”units of production to be processed.

The map you see is a blueprint of this oppression. Each red pin is not a data point; it is a prison, a place of death. Each grey pin is a processing facility where their dismembered bodies are packaged for consumption. Each violet pin is a laboratory where their cries go unheard behind sterile walls.

We publish this information not as a passive list, but as a call to conscience. We believe that every cage, every transport truck, every kill floor is a moral atrocity.

There is no justification for this tyranny. There is no humane way to take a life that does not want to be taken. The only just, the only compassionate, the only moral response is total liberation.

Our goal is not reform. It is not bigger cages or "less cruel" methods. Our goal is empty cages.

We see you. We hear your cries. We will not stop until every cage is empty.
