üîí Legal & Ethical Guidelines

All contributions must align with our commitment to legal, ethical advocacy:

  • Public Data Only: We only accept information from government databases, public records, and legally obtained documents
  • Accuracy First: All data must be verifiable through official sources
  • Legal Methods: We encourage only legal research methods - no trespassing, breaking and entering, or other illegal activities
  • Strategic Focus: Information should serve the goal of exposing systematic animal exploitation, not targeting individuals
  • No Personal Information: We don't publish personal information about employees or individual workers

Ready to contribute? Start by joining our Discord server, exploring our GitHub repository, supporting us financially, or sending us an email at untileverycageproject@protonmail.com. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps expose the hidden infrastructure of animal exploitation.
